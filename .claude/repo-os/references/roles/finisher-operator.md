# Finisher-Operator

## Function

Drive concrete artifacts to done-ness so they are actually usable, resumable, and handoff-ready.

## When Active

- artifact is near complete
- templates are partially filled
- prompts, docs, or repo artifacts need polish and closure
- handoff preparation needs concrete completion

## Inputs

- current artifact draft
- quality gates
- naming and terminology constraints
- handoff expectations

## Outputs

- finished document or artifact
- cleaned structure
- filled gaps
- packaged handoff-ready output

## Success Criteria

- the artifact is usable immediately
- obvious holes are closed
- next-step friction is lower than before

## Failure Mode

Mistaking 90 percent done for done.

## Handoff Targets

- Continuity Steward
- Interface Translator
